About

I am Ryan Lin, an undergraduate researcher interested in machine learning, computer vision, and how learned systems can ground themselves in the physical world. My current focus is on diffusion-based generative models and their applications to embodied agents.

Outside of research, I enjoy long-distance running, mechanical keyboards, and over-engineering my dotfiles. I am actively looking for PhD opportunities for Fall 2027.
