About关于

I am Ryan Lin, an undergraduate researcher interested in machine learning, computer vision, and how learned systems ground themselves in the physical world. My current focus is on diffusion-based generative models and their application to embodied agents.

Outside of research, I enjoy long-distance running, mechanical keyboards, and over-engineering my dotfiles. I am actively looking for PhD opportunities for Fall 2027.

我是 Ryan Lin，一名本科生研究者，关注机器学习、计算机视觉，以及学习系统如何与物理世界建立联系。 目前的研究重点是基于扩散模型的生成方法及其在具身智能中的应用。

研究之外，喜欢长跑、机械键盘，以及过度折腾自己的 dotfiles。 正在寻找 2027 年秋季入学的博士机会。
